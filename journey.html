My Journey

Steering as best as I can on this bumpy road called life

2013

Graduated high school and started IT college.

2014

Started working a part time job as a merchandiser for a big company while still studying.

2015

Work was booming so I dropped out of college to open my own business. Acquired more contracts with multiple companies and worked as their merchandiser.

2016

The idea of creating a website was always in the back of my mind. Started playing around with WordPress and Shopify creating some simple sites that were generating low profit. At the same time found my love for vector graphics so I started learning graphic design.

2017

Brainstormed for ideas with a good friend of mine, which eventually led to creating a website for selling slow feeding dog bowls called "Slowgie". Also started selling my graphic designs on print on demand sites.

2018

Went back to work on my IT degree while still managing my buisness and the website. We sold the initial stock of bowls and decided to focus on other more profitable ventures.

2020

Went abroad for a semester to the Pomeranian University in Slupsk (Poland). At the moment I am finishing up my IT degree while focusing on front end development.

2021 - Future

Get a steady job as a junior front end developer and work my way up!
