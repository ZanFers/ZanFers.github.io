My Journey

A never-ending one

2013

Graduated high school and started IT college.

2014

Started working as a commercial sales representative for multiple companies while still studying.

2015

Work was booming so I dropped out of college to open my own business, acquiring more contracts to work as a sales representative.

2016

The idea of creating a website was always in the back of my head. Started playing around with WordPress and Shopify creating some simple sites that were generating low profit. At the same time found my love for vector graphics started learning graphic design.

2017

Brainstormed for ideas with a good friend of mine, which eventually led to creating a website for selling slow feeding dog bowls called Slowgie. Started selling my graphic designs on print on demand sites.

2018

Went back to work on my IT degree while still managing my buisness and the website.

2020

Went abroad to study IT at the Pomeranian University in Slupsk for 6 months. At the moment I am finishing up my IT degree and focusing on front end development.
